about Feelogg


People want to be happy. Feelogg's mission is to make people happy.


Feelogg is a free, cross-channel, short-message diary platform that leverages your existing social networks and online activity to track your mood over time, and generate insights to help you understand what makes you happy or sad. Feelogg enables you to share your mood with friends across social networks and to get supported, as well as to support your happy and gloomy friends (which Feelogg automatically identifies even if they donâ€™t use Feelogg yet). The Feelogg platform can be accessed on Facebook, as a mobile app on iPhone/ iPad and Android, and on feelogg.com.


Feelogg is a privately-held company headquartered in Netanya, Israel.
